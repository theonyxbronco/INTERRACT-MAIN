Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 8, 2025

At Interract, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Contact us through our website
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Request information about our services
  • Submit inquiries or feedback

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website information
  • Device information

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze how our website is used. Specifically, we use the following tools:


Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This service collects information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • How you arrived at our website (referral source)
  • Your approximate geographic location
  • Device and browser information
  • Website performance metrics

Hotjar

We use Hotjar to understand user behavior and improve our website experience. Hotjar may collect:

  • Heatmaps showing where users click, move, and scroll
  • Session recordings of user interactions (anonymized)
  • Feedback and survey responses
  • Device and browser information

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences or opt out of these services:

  • Google Analytics: Opt-out here
  • Hotjar: Opt-out here

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • To improve our website and user experience through analytics
  • To understand user behavior and optimize our website design
  • To analyze website traffic, performance, and usage patterns
  • To identify technical issues and improve website functionality
  • To send you updates about our services (with your consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations

Analytics and User Experience

We use Google Analytics and Hotjar to:

  • Track website performance and identify popular content
  • Understand user journeys and navigation patterns
  • Identify areas for website improvement
  • Optimize page loading times and user interface design
  • Generate anonymized reports for internal business purposes

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our website (Google Analytics, Hotjar)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • In connection with a business transfer or merger

Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics: Operated by Google LLC. Data is processed according to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Hotjar: Operated by Hotjar Ltd. Data is processed according to Hotjar's Privacy Policy: https://www.hotjar.com/legal/policies/privacy/

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent
